2026 ATS Optimization Standards:
Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) in 2026 require a reverse-chronological, single-column layout in standard system fonts like Arial or Calibri at 10-12pt. You must strictly avoid tables, text boxes, graphics, or custom icons. Use standard headers such as 'Work Experience', 'Education', and 'Skills' rather than creative variants like 'My Journey' or 'Toolbox'. Do not stuff keywords randomly; use the exact wording from the job description naturally within the context of your achievements. Both .docx and text-selectable PDFs are accepted by modern ATS.
